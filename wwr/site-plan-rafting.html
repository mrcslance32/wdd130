Overview

Purpose

Increase revenue by showing potential customers in our area that our company exists. Provide contact information to allow potential customers to reach out an book appointment. Show different services we provide.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Adventureous, active, outdoorsey types. Largely college aged - 40ish. Most likely also hikers and involved in other extreme sports.

What do they want? To find out door activities they can do with their friends.

How will my audience usually access my site? Almost exclusivley on a mobile format. Most likely an iPhone.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? Pricing, services, contact information, location.
